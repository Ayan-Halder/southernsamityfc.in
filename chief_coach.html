CHIEF COACHES

CHIEF COACH (2010-11)
NIVALAPPIL MANI VIJAYAN
Inivalappil Mani Vijayan is known as KALO HARIN.IM Vijayan is an Arjuna award winner.He was a former professional Indian football player, who was born on 25th April 1969 in Thrissur, Kerala.
CHIEF COACH (2011-12)
carlos pereira
In 2011-2012, he became chief coach of the Southern Samity Club, which achieved a great deal of success under his guidance. The Brazilian with his vast experience and expertise pulling up to making a renowned club.
CHIEF COACH (2012-13)
shabbir ali
Shabbir Ali, (born 26 January 1956) is an Indian football manager and former player.He was awarded the Dhyan Chand Award, the highest award in Indian sports for lifetime achievement, given by Government of India in 2011.
CHIEF COACH (2013-14)
subrata Bhattacharya
Subrata Bhattacharya played for 17 years in Mohun Bagan (1974–1990). He became the Captain of Mohun Bagan in 1977; Mohun Bagan won the triple crown that season and became the first club to do so in Indian football history.
CHIEF COACH (2014-15)
subrata Bhattacharya jr. (patla)
He is a talented Indian Coach. He has joined Southern Samity as a chief coach in the year 2014-15.
CHIEF COACH (2015-16)
raghu nandy
He is a renowned name in Kolkata's football as a coach . He has Joined Souhern Samity Club as Chief Coach in the year 2015-16.
CHIEF COACH (2016-19)
Hemanta Dora
Hemanta Dora (born 4TH December 1974) is a former Indian International football player who played as a goalkeeper. Dora has played for Mohun Bagan and won the 1997-98 National Football League.
ASST. COACHING STAFF (2014-till today)
utpal Mukherjee
Mr. Utpal Mukherjee took his charge as youth development coach from 2014. He joined southern samity club as an asst. Coaching staff and till today he is our youth development coach.
ASST. & GK COACH (2013-till today)
SNEHASHISH CHAKRABORTY
Mr. Snehashish Chakraborty Played for Jadavpur University from 1983 to 1986. Youth India in the year 1986 held at Riyadh. Then join Aryan Club in the year 1987 played upto 1988.
CHIEF COACH (2019-20)
MEHTAB HOSSIN
The Great Player Mr. Mehetab Hossain Chief Coach of our Club Southern Samity season 2019-2020. Mehtab Hossain (born 5 September 1985) is a retired Indian professional footballer who played as a defensive midfielder.
