SOUTHERN SAMITY FC

OUR OFFICIALS

PRESIDENT
Asraf Ali Shaikh
The ambitious man, a person with clear aspiration Capt. Asraf was introduced to the club in 2010 for the very first time.
SR. VICE PRESIDENT
Dr. Pranab Kumar Mukherjee
Dr. Pranab Kumar Mukherjee, the Senior Vice President of Southern Samity FC is well known physician in the city. And on the the other hand he is an Ex - Sports person who has left a glorious mark on the pages of the sport's history.
VICE PRESIDENT
DR. TIRTHANKAR BAGCHI
Dr. Tirthankar Bagchi, General Manager Medical Services & Medical Superintendent, AMRI Hospital, Kolkata has become an integral and inseparable part of Southern Samity Club as a life member within a short period of time.
VICE PRESIDENT
Lalit Sharma
Lalit Sharma, Vice President of Southern Samity, is an entrepreneur and former martial arts enthusiast. He received a Black belt in Karate while in school at the age of fourteen.
CHAIR PERSON
JINIA ROY CHOWDHURY
Jinia Roy Chowdhury started her career in the field of acting and modeling and for her, football was a natural passion. In 2018, she became involved with the prestigious Southern Samity Football Club.
GENERAL SECRETARY
Sourav Pal
With an immense personality and desire to become a footballer, Sourav Pal then joined Southern Samity. He handled the duties of General Secretary day-in and day-out for many years.
FOOTBALL SECRETARY
Kushal Banerjee
The association between Kushal Banerjee and football dates back to 1979 when he joined Kalighat Milani Football Club. He played for Calcutta Customs Club from 1980 to 1998, as well as a season for Rajasthan Club in 1991.
C E O
Amit Sen
Mr. Amit Sen is the chief executive officer (CEO) of Southern Samity Football Club. He joined Southern Samity in 2010, pursuing an interest in football that started at an early age.
YOUTH DEVELOPMENT
RAJDEEP DUTTA
Rajdeep Dutta has nearly ten years of experience as a football development officer working with young players. He is a sports fanatic with an affinity for football.

ABOUT

For the first time in 1945, Southern Samity FC made an enormous mark in Kolkata. Southern Samity was the runner-up in the 2016 I-League 2nd Division, much to the delight of the fans. The club has a clear mission and goals that it hopes to achieve shortly.
