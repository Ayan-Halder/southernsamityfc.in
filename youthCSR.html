YOUTH DEVELOPMENT
Youth Development will be a key area to work on when it comes to the long term success of the club. This will create a supply line of young players who can make it to the senior team.
PAY-&-PLAY MODEL
  • Generate Revenue
  • Get a pool of young players
  • Scholarships for special players
  • Youth team to play in the AIFF youth leagues.
  • Partcipation in Baby Leagues
  • Participation in Junior and Sub-junior Championships
Corporate Social Responsibility
Corporate Social Responsibility initiative in football is a great example for corporates on how to leverage football to not only engage with the communities that the company operates in, but also work towards a larger dream of the country - to see India on the world football map.

ABOUT

For the first time in 1945, Southern Samity FC made an enormous mark in Kolkata. Southern Samity was the runner-up in the 2016 I-League 2nd Division, much to the delight of the fans. The club has a clear mission and goals that it hopes to achieve shortly.
