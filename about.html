SOUTHERN SAMITY FC

OURVISION

To get an identity of the prime club in India. Door to door development, consistently nurturing young guts in the tag of “FUTURE FOOTBALLER” and this process remains unstoppable

OURMISSION

To get the levels up, to be a part of the AFC Cup to crown the I-League trophy by 2022-23. “Southern Samity Community” has formed with this initiative of all, to take this journey forward and eminent names such as Mr. Sukumar Samajpati, Mr. Subrata Bhattacharya, Mr. I.M.Vijayan, Mr. Krishnendu Roy, and Mr. Hemanta Dora are leading to achieve the task.

HISTORY

The city of joy, Kolkata has stood to establish Southern Samity Football Club in 1945, a new dawn, an inspiration came across the fans of Bengal. Sri Uday Chand Mahakab, Maharaja of Burdwan came to the spotlight and build the heart of Southern, moreover the club carrying the legacy over India. Southern Samity FC entered into the professional football league as a part of the Indian Football Association for the very first time. On a very good note Southern bags the first trophy into the cabinet to the winning of Bengal Soccer League in 1961. A dream on top of the head to make a pathway in Volleyball, accordingly, the club came forward and remarkably they won State League Championship as well as State Knockout Title in a row.

ABOUT

For the first time in 1945, Southern Samity FC made an enormous mark in Kolkata. Southern Samity was the runner-up in the 2016 I-League 2nd Division, much to the delight of the fans. The club has a clear mission and goals that it hopes to achieve shortly.
